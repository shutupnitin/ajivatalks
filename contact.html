Ajiva Talks is open to thoughtful conversations.

We interview writers, artists, editors, thinkers, founders, and entrepreneurs whose work is shaped by ideas, lived experience, and a clear point of view. Our focus is not on hype or visibility, but on substance — the thinking behind the work, the personal histories that inform it, and the questions that continue to shape it.

If you are an author, creative practitioner, cultural worker, founder, or entrepreneur with a story grounded in craft, values, or long-term vision, we welcome you to reach out.

We are particularly interested in

Areas of Focus

Authors and poets
Editors, publishers, and translators
Artists, filmmakers, and cultural practitioners
Founders and entrepreneurs building thoughtful, values-driven work
Thinkers working at the intersection of culture, identity, philosophy, or technology

All interviews are long-form and editorially curated. We do not conduct promotional features, press releases, or sponsored content.

How to reach us

For interview requests, editorial inquiries, or general correspondence, please email:

hello@ajivatalks.in

If you are pitching yourself or someone else for an interview, include:

  • A brief introduction
  • Relevant work or background
  • Why you believe the conversation would be meaningful for Ajiva Talks
We read every message, though replies may take time. Ajiva Talks operates independently and prioritizes careful curation over volume. Thank you for your interest in thoughtful dialogue.
