Ajay K Pandey — On Love, Loss, Writing & New Beginnings

By Ajiva Talks
•
Interviews / Personal Essays
•
— min read

Ajay K Pandey — IT professional, traveller, counselor and bestselling author — writes from life. His debut memoir-style novel You Are The Best Wife touched readers deeply; his follow-up An Unexpected Gift explores finding love again. In this conversation he shares the origins of his writing, his hobbies, and how travel and reading inform his outlook.

Quick facts

Works: You Are The Best Wife; An Unexpected Gift

Lives: Pune (Cognizant)

Hobbies: Travel, trekking, reading

Donate

1. Can you tell us about your journey as a writer? How did you start your career in writing?
Ajay K Pandey: My journey as a writer began with my debut book titled “You Are the Best Wife,” a deeply personal story based on my life experiences with my beloved wife. The book serves as a tribute to her and the beautiful bond we shared, even amidst challenges and heartache. Initially, I wrote it to cherish and preserve our memories. The response from readers — heartfelt messages and appreciation — inspired me to continue writing and exploring themes of love, hope and resilience.
2. Apart from writing, what other things do you like to do and what are your hobbies?
Ajay K Pandey: I also work as a Relationship Counsellor, helping people navigate relationship challenges. I love nature — travelling and trekking are favourite pastimes. I find solace in simple pleasures too: a cup of tea and pakoras by a river or in the mountains is pure joy. These quiet moments recharge me and often spark ideas for my writing.
3. As someone inspired by travelling, trekking and reading, how have these hobbies influenced your perspective on life and work?
Ajay K Pandey: Routine can make us complacent. Travelling exposes you to new cultures and people and reveals things that unite us. It broadens the mind and helps your authentic self surface. Travel can be a great stress-buster — when stressed, a change of scene often helps more than anything. Trekking teaches resilience: face obstacles head-on and enjoy the view at the top.
4. Your debut novel, "You Are The Best Wife," was deeply personal. What motivated you to share your life events, and how did its success impact you?
Ajay K Pandey: I never planned to write a novel. After my dear wife Bhavna passed away, I was in deep pain and started writing to channel that grief. It slowly became a book. The memory of Bhavna inspired me — I wrote to preserve her memory and perhaps inspire others. The book’s success and the messages it brought were overwhelming; being called a bestselling author gives me a new purpose and joy, and it feels like I have done something meaningful for her memory.
5. We understand reading is significant in your life. Which books have influenced you most?
Ajay K Pandey: Two books that had a profound impact are *The Company of Women* and *The Kite Runner*. Their storytelling and emotional depth inspired me deeply.
6. Did you read a lot as a child? How much research did you do before writing your novels?
Ajay K Pandey: I wasn't a voracious reader as a child, though I loved writers like Premchand and Sarat Chandra. Before my first book, I learned a lot by reading widely — over 100 popular books in a year to study craft and structure. That reading helped me learn how to unfold events and write effectively.
7. What is your perception of relationships today? Can love last a lifetime?
Ajay K Pandey: Relationships are about emotions; caring is at their heart. Real love begins when attraction fades and deep care remains. Time is the test — when love grows with time, it’s real. I believe love can last a lifetime when it deepens through care and commitment.
