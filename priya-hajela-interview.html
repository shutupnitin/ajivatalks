Resilience, Partition & Human Spirit — A Conversation with Priya Hajela

Category: Literature / Author Interviews • Ajiva Talks

Priya Hajela’s writing is shaped by a life filled with cultural transitions, motherhood, immigration, and decades of corporate experience. From Ludhiana to American universities to returning to India, her journey informs her work with emotional depth and clarity. Her debut novel Ladies’ Tailor explores resilience, identity, friendship, and the legacy of partition, drawing from her MFA in Creative Writing and personal heritage.
