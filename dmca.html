DMCA Policy

Last Updated: 2025

Ajiva Talks respects the intellectual property rights of authors, creators, artists, and publishers. If you believe your copyrighted work has been used in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, you may file a DMCA request.

How to Submit a DMCA Takedown Request

Please include the following details in your email:

  • Your full legal name
  • Your contact information (email + optional phone)
  • Link to the original copyrighted work
  • Link to the infringing content on Ajiva Talks
  • A statement confirming you have a good-faith belief that the use is unauthorized
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information provided is accurate
  • Your electronic signature

Send DMCA Requests To:

Email: hello@ajivatalks.in

Important Notes

  • We may remove or disable content while reviewing your request.
  • We may forward your complaint to the individual who posted the content.
  • Submitting false claims is a legal offense.

Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed by mistake, you may file a counter-notice. It must include:

  • Your name & contact information
  • Identification of the content removed
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the removal was incorrect
  • Your consent to local court jurisdiction
  • Your electronic signature

We will restore content if legally justified.
