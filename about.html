About Ajiva Talks

Ajiva Talks is an independent literary and cultural platform dedicated to meaningful conversations. We spotlight authors, thinkers, artists, and voices who shape the creative landscape of India and beyond.

Our interviews are crafted with depth and intention — focusing not only on books, but on ideas, journeys, and the human experiences behind creative work.

Our Mission

  • To celebrate literature and storytelling
  • To bring thoughtful interviews to readers who value substance
  • To highlight emerging and established voices in India
  • To create a digital archive of honest, timeless conversations

What We Publish

  • Author Interviews
  • Editorial Narratives
  • Opinion & Thought Pieces
  • Cultural Commentary

Our Values

Ajiva Talks stands for sincerity, clarity, and compassion. Every interview is crafted to honor the author’s voice and enrich the reader’s understanding.

The Founder

Ajiva Talks was created with a love for books and a passion for authentic conversations. Our goal is to nurture a literary platform that feels human, honest, and beautifully designed.
