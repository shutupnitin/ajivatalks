About Ajiva Talks

Ajiva Talks is an independent literary and cultural platform dedicated to interviewing authors, thinkers, artists, and voices shaping India’s creative landscape.

Our mission is simple:
To preserve meaningful conversations and celebrate storytelling.

We publish:

  • Exclusive author interviews
  • Editorial commentary
  • Book-related features
  • Culture & thought pieces

Ajiva Talks is built for readers who love depth, nuance, and high-quality independent journalism.
